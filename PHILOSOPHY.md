## 🏗️ The Build Philosophy

1. **Iterate aggressively.** V57 exists because V1 existed. Don't wait for perfect — ship and version.

2. **Document everything.** The framework is in the docs as much as in the code. Future-you needs the context.

3. **Build for your own stack.** Not what tutorials say. What works on your device, in your environment, with your tools.

4. **Version numbers protect you.** `bashrc_v10.sh` instead of just `bashrc` — never overwrite, always increment.

5. **The alias is the UX.** A clean alias like `station` or `dl` IS the product experience.

6. **Dark humour is a coping mechanism with output.** If you can make a joke about it, you're processing it. If you can build a tool about it, you've transcended it.
